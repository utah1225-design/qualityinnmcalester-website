Frequently asked questions

Answers, Directly

Where can I park a semi-truck or RV near McAlester OK? ▾
Free oversized vehicle parking is available at Quality Inn & Suites McAlester, 400 S George Nigh Expy, on US Highway 69. The lot accommodates semi-trucks, tractor-trailers, RVs, motorcoaches, and all oversized rigs at no charge for hotel guests. McAlester's largest on-site lot — the only hotel in McAlester with dedicated oversized vehicle parking. Non-guest parking available at $15/day or $75/week. MCAAP is approximately 10–15 minutes away via Hwy 270.
Does Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 serve a free hot breakfast? ▾
Yes — free full hot breakfast is served daily from 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM for all registered guests. Menu includes: scrambled eggs, sausage, waffles, pancakes, fresh fruit, oatmeal, juices, coffee, breads, and bagels. This is a full cooked meal — not grab-and-go packaged items. No advance reservation required.
Does every room have a refrigerator and microwave? ▾
Yes — every guest room includes a mini-fridge, microwave, and in-room safe as standard. No upgrade required. Additional amenities in every room: Smart TV with Netflix and Amazon Prime built-in, soundproofing installed, free Wi-Fi, coffee maker, work desk, and hair dryer. Six select kitchenette suites also have a stovetop and full-size refrigerator.
Does Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 offer weekly or monthly extended stay rates? ▾
Yes — extended stay rates are available for 7-night and 28-night minimum stays. Available for MCAAP contractors (promo: MCAAP), corporate crews, government and military personnel, and individual long-term guests. Six kitchenette suites with stovetop are available. Call (918) 426-8091 for current pricing and availability.
Where should MCAAP contractors stay near McAlester Army Ammunition Plant? ▾
Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 is approximately minutes away from MCAAP via Hwy 270. Use promo code MCAAP at booking for the Mission Ready Rate. Includes free hot breakfast, free oversized parking, refrigerator and microwave in every room, 24/7 front desk, and daily housekeeping. Tax-exempt billing available for qualifying government contractors.
Is Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 pet-friendly? ▾
Yes — pet-friendly with a $15 per night per pet fee. Pets must be declared at check-in. Service animals accommodated per ADA requirements. Contact (918) 426-8091 before arrival to confirm pet room availability.
What are the check-in and check-out times? ▾
Check-in is 3:00 PM. Check-out is 11:00 AM. Front desk is staffed 24 hours, 7 days a week. Early check-in and late check-out may be available — call (918) 426-8091 in advance to request. Address: 400 S George Nigh Expy, McAlester, OK 74501.
Does Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 have Netflix and streaming TV? ▾
Yes — Netflix and Amazon Prime are built directly into every room's Smart TV. Use your own Netflix and Amazon Prime accounts — sign in once and resume any show right where you left off at home. On-Demand DirectTV is also available. No additional streaming fee. All room types include this as a standard amenity.
How does Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 compare to other hotels in McAlester? ▾
Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 is the highest-rated hotel in McAlester at 4.6★ on Google with 767 reviews. We offer a free full hot breakfast daily (eggs, sausage, waffles, pancakes), free Wi-Fi with no tiers, a 24/7 staffed front desk, Netflix and Amazon Prime built into every Smart TV, and McAlester's largest oversized parking lot — free for all guests.
Does Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69 offer government and military per diem rates? ▾
Yes — government and military per diem rates are accepted. Valid government-issued ID or military credentials required at check-in. Tax-exempt billing available for qualifying agencies. GSA per diem schedules for Pittsburg County, Oklahoma honored. Call (918) 426-8091 to confirm eligibility.
Is there a 24-hour fitness center at Quality Inn & Suites McAlester on Hwy 69? ▾
Yes — the on-site fitness center is open 24 hours, 7 days a week at no additional charge. Guest laundry is also available on-site 24/7. Both facilities are complimentary for registered guests.
