Lake Eufaula

Oklahoma’s largest lake — boating, fishing, swimming, and water sports. One of the state’s most scenic outdoor destinations.

Robbers Cave State Park

Hiking, rappelling, equestrian trails, and camping in the San Bois Mountains. Popular for families and outdoor enthusiasts.

~20 miles north

Choctaw Casino McAlester

Full-service casino with gaming, dining, and entertainment. One of McAlester’s top entertainment destinations.

~5 miles south

Southeast Expo Center

Major regional events venue — livestock shows, fairs, trade shows, rodeos, and community events throughout the year.

~3 miles west via Hwy 270

Scottish Rite Masonic Temple

One of McAlester’s most distinctive landmarks. Notable historic architecture worth seeing in the heart of downtown.
