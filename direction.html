Driving directions

Routes from Major Cities

From Tulsa, OK

~90 minutes · ~90 miles south

  1. Take US-75 South from Tulsa toward Henryetta
  2. Merge onto US-69 South at Henryetta
  3. Continue south on US-69 into McAlester (~40 mi)
  4. Hotel is on the right at S George Nigh Expy, next to Walmart

From Oklahoma City, OK

~2 hours · ~120 miles east

  1. Take I-40 East from OKC toward Henryetta
  2. Exit at US-75 South (Henryetta) OR continue to US-270 East
  3. Follow US-270 East into McAlester
  4. Turn onto US-69, hotel on left at S George Nigh Expy

From Dallas, TX

~3.5 hours · ~200 miles north

  1. Take US-69 North from Dallas area (Sherman/Denison)
  2. Cross into Oklahoma, continue north through Durant and Atoka
  3. Continue north on US-69 into McAlester
  4. Hotel is on left side at S George Nigh Expy

From Fort Smith, AR

~1.5 hours · ~90 miles west

  1. Take US-271 West from Fort Smith into Oklahoma
  2. Continue west to Poteau, then take US-270 West
  3. US-270 West leads directly into McAlester
  4. Turn onto US-69 South, hotel on right at S George Nigh Expy
