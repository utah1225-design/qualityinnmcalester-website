FAQ

Parking Questions

Is parking free for hotel guests? ▼
Yes — parking is completely free for all hotel guests with no nightly vehicle fee, no pre-registration, and no size restrictions. Semi-trucks, RVs, trailers, motorcoaches, and passenger vehicles are all welcome at no charge.
Can non-guests use the parking lot? ▼
Yes — standalone parking is available for non-guests at $15 per day or $75 per week. Contact the front desk at (918) 426-8091 to arrange. Payment required at check-in.
Are there hookups or shore power for RVs? ▼
No. The parking lot does not have electrical hookups, water connections, or shore power. It is a standard paved and gravel surface lot for overnight and extended parking. RV guests are welcome to park and use all hotel amenities including the free hot breakfast.
Is there a height restriction or weight limit? ▼
No posted height restriction or weight limit. The lot is a surface lot with direct Hwy 69 access and no low-clearance structures. Full-size semi-trucks and 18-wheelers park here regularly.
